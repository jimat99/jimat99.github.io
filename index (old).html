My 3x3 PLL Algorithms :)

Aa Permutation (A-Perm)

Goal: Swaps three corners counter-clockwise and swaps two adjacent edges.

Setup:

x R2' D2' R U R' D2' R U' R x'

Algorithm:

x R' U R' D2 R U' R' D2 R2 x'

Ab Permutation (A-Perm)

Goal: Swaps three corners clockwise and swaps two adjacent edges.

Setup:

x R' U R' D2' R U' R' D2' R2' x'

Algorithm:

y' x L U' L D2 L' U L D2 L2

E Permutation (E-Perm)

Goal: Diagonally swaps all 4 corner pieces and 4 edge pieces in an "X" shape.

Setup:

x' D R U R' D' R U' R' D R U' R' D' R U R' x y'

Algorithm:

z U2 R2 F (R U R' U' R U R' U' R U R' U') F' R2 U2 z'
R2 U F' (R' U R U' R' U R U' R' U R U') F U' R2

F Permutation (F-Perm)
